Nexus Agile Project Management

Form Project

Establish the project framework and initial setup to begin work.

Initiate Nexus Integration Team

Assemble the Nexus Integration Team to coordinate and integrate efforts across multiple Scrum teams.

Define Nexus Goal

Establish a clear, overarching goal for the Nexus framework to ensure alignment and focus across all teams.

Product Backlog Creation

Create the Product Backlog by defining and prioritizing initial features and requirements to guide future development.

Sprint Planning

Plan the work for the upcoming Sprint by selecting and organizing items from the Product Backlog.

Sprint Backlog

The Sprint Backlog is a crucial artifact created during Sprint Planning, consisting of selected Product Backlog items and tasks necessary to achieve the Sprint Goal.

Daily Nexus

Conduct daily synchronization meetings to align teams and address any dependencies or blockers.

Nexus Sprint Review

Review the completed work of the Sprint, gather feedback, and evaluate if the Sprint goals were met.

Nexus Sprint Retrospective

Reflect on the Sprint to identify lessons learned and implement improvements for future Sprints.

Nexus Sprint Backlog Refinement

Refine the Sprint Backlog based on retrospective insights and review outcomes to prepare for the next Sprint Planning.
