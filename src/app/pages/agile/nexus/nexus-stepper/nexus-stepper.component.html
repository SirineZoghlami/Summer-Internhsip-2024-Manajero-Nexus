Nexus Agile Project Management

Form Project

Initiate Nexus Integration Team

Form the Nexus Integration Team responsible for coordinating and integrating work across multiple Scrum teams.

Define Nexus Goal

Set a clear goal for the Nexus framework to align efforts across all teams.

Product Backlog Refinement

Refine and prioritize the product backlog to ensure it is ready for the next sprint.

Sprint Planning

Organize and plan the work for the upcoming sprint based on the refined backlog.

Daily Nexus

Conduct daily synchronization meetings to align teams and address any dependencies or blockers.

Nexus Sprint Review

Review the work completed in the sprint, gather feedback, and assess whether sprint objectives were achieved.

Nexus Sprint Retrospective

Reflect on the sprint to identify and implement improvements for future sprints.

Nexus Sprint Backlog Refinement

Refine the sprint backlog based on insights from the retrospective and review to prepare for the next sprint planning.
